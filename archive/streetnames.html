User Guide

1. Drawing

  • Select Highway or Street.
  • Click to place points.
  • Auto-Smooth will curve the line for you.

2. Editing

  • Click Select / Edit.
  • Select a road. The Red Dashed Line is your editable skeleton.
  • Move the white dots to reshape the curve.
  • Use the Make Smooth/Straight button to toggle curves.

3. Labels

  • Click Place Label and click the map.
  • Select the purple dot to Move or Rotate the text.
